This is a user-aggregated board game.


The point of the game is to move around the board 1 time. Users submit their ideas for the best cards, and everyone can vote on the cards they think are the best.

Submitted cards start with a score of 1. Once the cards hit a score of -5, they are no longer used.

I've worked to bring two things I love to this website: user-aggregated content and boardgames. I hope you enjoy! Please submit any bugs or comments to projectbgame@gmail.com
